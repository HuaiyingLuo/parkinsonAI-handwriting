Welcome to HandSight

HandSight is designed to assist in the early diagnosis of Parkinson's Disease (PD) by analyzing hand-drawn images using AI.

Upload an image of a hand-drawn spiral or meandering drawing, and our AI model will predict whether the drawing indicates a positive or negative result for Parkinson's Disease.
